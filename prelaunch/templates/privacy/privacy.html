Effective Date: May 14, 2025

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

Welcome to Profitt. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to share or access trading insights as a Trader or Learner.

Information We Collect

.1 Personal Information (Provided by You)

  • Your name, email address, and account credentials (securely stored and encrypted)
  • No financial or payment information is collected during the pre-launch phase
  • Any content you post, including market analyses, TradingView links, or comments

.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Your IP address, device type, browser type, and system logs
  • Platform usage data (e.g., login timestamps, pages viewed)
  • Cookies and other tracking technologies (see Section 5)

How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the platform
  • Facilitate communication between Traders and Learners
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Share updates, service notifications, and promotional content (if opted-in)

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share limited information with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., analytics, hosting, future payment processors)
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with law or prevent misuse

Data Security

We implement best practices to protect your data. While we strive for full security, no system is completely immune to risks. We recommend using strong passwords and enabling any available account protection tools.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Profitt uses cookies to enhance your user experience and track activity on the platform. You may adjust your cookie settings in your browser, but this may affect functionality.

Your Rights & Choices

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms (e.g., TradingView). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy as our platform evolves. The latest version will always be published on our site with an updated effective date.
